Classic Lasagna

Description

We're going to be honest: making lasagna is no walk in the park. It can be a challenging dish to make, depending on your level of cooking experience. However, if you stick to the recipe, the end result will make it all worth it. This lasagna is decadent, savory, and full of flavor. You will be happy you spent the time making this dish.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomato
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 tubes tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Seasonings: Fresh parsley, dried basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, black pepper
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta
  • 1 egg
  • Onion and Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, garlic until meat is brown
  2. Add in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water
  3. Season meat sauce with sugar and seasonings
  4. Let simmer, while covered, for 1.5 hours
  5. Cook lasagna noodles as per box instructions
  6. Combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and salt in a bowl
  7. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  8. Layer lasagna as follows: Meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella, parmesan
  9. Repeat until there is no more room in tray
  10. Bake for total of 50 minutes: First 25 with foil, last 25 without foil
  11. Let cool and enjoy!
