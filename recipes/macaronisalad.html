Macaroni Salad

Description

This macaroni salad recipe is super easy to make. It is very quick too, making it ideal for those times when you're in a pinch. The recipe we have listed below is completely vegetarian, but feel free to add your own personal touch with whatever fix-ins you like!

This dish is perfect for potlucks, picnics, barbecues, and really any other occasion you can think of. It is a cold and refreshing dish fit for a summer day. If you have any extra leftovers (you likely won't), make sure to store in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Noodles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Salt and Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni noodles in salted water as per box instructions
  2. Mix in 3 parts mayonnaise, 1 part mustard
  3. Add the sugar, vinegar, salt and pepper to the dressing
  4. Dice the vegetables to your desired consistency and thickness
  5. Stir in the macaroni and vegetables. Mix thoroughly
  6. Taste for seasoning. Adjust if necessary
  7. Enjoy!
