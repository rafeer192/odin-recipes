Restaurant Style Slaw

Description

This restaurant style coleslaw is a perfect side kick to just about any salty, savory dish. Its sweet hints and cool temperature serve as a delicioius palate cleanser and side dish. It's extremely easy to make, with no real cooking required!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz bag of coleslaw mix veggies
  • 2 tbsp diced onion
  • 2/3 cup creamy salad dressing
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp poppy seeds
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Combined diced onions with coleslaw mix in large bowl
  2. Whisk together salad dressing, sugar, oil, vinegar, poppy seeds and salt thoroughly in separate bowl
  3. Pour dressing over coleslaw, taste for seasoning and adjust if desired
  4. Sit patiently while the slaw chills for 2 hours in the fridge
  5. Enjoy!
